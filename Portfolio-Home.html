Image Project

The client is creating a website on the topic of parenting. The general idea of the client is parents are an important influence on a child’s whole life; however, not everyone knows how to be a good parent.

- Create a content-relevant and attractive banner on homepage, highlight parenting in a soothing way and be natural to reader.

- Create an icon image to serve as a site logo for each page. The icon is flat looking, with a minimum number of colors used, and needs to reflect or embody the banner idea in a visually simplified way.

- Use techniques and effect in photoshop with two original image from camera/cell phone to create a artistic final image.
